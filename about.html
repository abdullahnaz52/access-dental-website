Our Mission

Redefining Dental Care in Kashmir

Access Dental Care & Orthodontics was established in 2014 at Anzimar, Khanyar, Srinagar with a clear purpose: to end the need for Kashmiri patients to travel to Delhi, Chandigarh, or abroad for high-quality dental treatment. Our founders — Dr. Sameer Malik and Dr. Nida Lanker — both trained at top institutions nationally and internationally before returning to their roots to serve their community.

We invest continuously in the latest dental technology — from digital X-rays and 3D imaging to rotary endodontic systems and modern orthodontic materials — so that the care you receive in Srinagar matches what you would find at the best clinics in India's metro cities.

Our clinic follows strict sterilisation and infection control protocols that meet international standards, ensuring complete safety and peace of mind for every patient. Every instrument is sterilised, every surface disinfected, and every procedure conducted with the utmost hygiene.

The Specialists

Meet the Doctors

Orthodontist & Implantologist

Dr. Sameer Malik

Dr. Sameer Malik is the lead orthodontist and implantologist at Access Dental Care. After completing his BDS from Government Dental College Srinagar, he pursued his MDS in Orthodontics from RGUHS, followed by the prestigious MFDS qualification from the Royal College of Surgeons Edinburgh — one of the most respected dental credentials in the world. He further completed a Fellowship at the Hospital for Sick Children (Sick Kids) in Toronto, Canada, gaining exposure to cutting-edge pediatric and interceptive orthodontics.

He has been practising in Srinagar since 2014 (Dental Council Regn. A-2294) and is a Member of the Indian Society of Oral Implantology, performing complex implant cases alongside comprehensive orthodontic treatment.

MFDS RCS Edinburgh, UK

General Dentist

Dr. Nida Lanker

Dr. Nida Lanker is a highly accomplished general dentist whose academic excellence was recognised with the prestigious University Gold Medal in BDS from Government Dental College Srinagar. Her passion for dentistry extends beyond clinical practice — she combines academic rigour with genuine compassion for her patients, making her particularly effective with children and patients who experience dental anxiety.

She completed her House Job in Oral Surgery at GDC Srinagar, gaining specialised skills in tooth extractions, surgical procedures, and oral health management. Registered with the Dental Council since 2017 (Regn. A-3341), she handles all general dentistry, preventive care, and complex oral surgery cases at the clinic.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Safety First

Every instrument is sterilised, every surface disinfected. We follow international infection control protocols to ensure complete safety for patients and staff alike.

Compassionate Care

We understand that dental visits can be stressful. Every patient is met with warmth, patience, and genuine concern — we never rush and always listen.

Evidence-Based Practice

All our treatments are grounded in the latest clinical research. We regularly update our skills and technology to ensure you receive the most effective, proven treatments available.

Transparent Communication

We explain your diagnosis, treatment options, and costs clearly before beginning any procedure. Informed patients make better decisions and experience better outcomes.

Community Commitment

As a locally owned clinic, we are deeply committed to improving oral health awareness across Srinagar and the Kashmir Valley, including outreach and patient education programmes.

Advanced Technology

Digital X-rays, 3D imaging, rotary endodontic systems, and modern orthodontic materials enable us to deliver precision treatment that was previously unavailable in Kashmir.

Your Health Is Our Priority
