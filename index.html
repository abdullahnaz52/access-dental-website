About Us

Access Dental Care & Orthodontics offers a wide range of oral health services especially Orthodontic treatment. Other treatments including dental implants, root canal, crowns tooth extraction, teeth whitening, veneers are also provided.

The doctors are professional with good knowledge. Customised treatment is given to kids with special care needs. You can directly visit or call to book an appointment.

Our clinic is equipped with modern technology and follows the highest standards of hygiene and sterilization to ensure the safety of our patients.

Dental Implants
