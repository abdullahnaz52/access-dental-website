Redefining Dental Care
in Kashmir

Access Dental Care & Orthodontics offers a wide range of oral health services especially Orthodontic treatment. Other treatments including dental implants, root canal, crowns, tooth extraction, teeth whitening, and veneers are also provided.

Our doctors are professional with deep expertise. Customised treatment is given to kids with special care needs. Modern technology meets compassionate care at every visit.

Our clinic follows the highest standards of hygiene and sterilization, ensuring complete safety for every patient.

What We Offer

Our Services

Orthodontic Treatment

Comprehensive braces and aligner treatments for all ages to achieve perfectly aligned teeth and a confident smile.

Dental Implants

Permanent solution for missing teeth that look, feel, and function exactly like natural teeth.

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening services to brighten your smile dramatically and safely.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless root canal procedures to save infected teeth and relieve discomfort effectively.

Crowns & Bridges

Restore damaged teeth or replace missing ones with precision custom-made crowns and bridges.

Pediatric Dentistry

Specialised gentle dental care for children in a friendly, comfortable, and stress-free environment.
