Orthodontic Treatment in Srinagar

Metal Braces · Ceramic Braces · Clear Aligners · Retainers

Orthodontic treatment corrects misaligned teeth (malocclusion), crowding, gaps, and bite problems using braces or clear aligners. At Access Dental Care, our specialist Dr. Sameer Malik — who holds an MDS in Orthodontics from RGUHS and a Fellowship from Sick Kids Hospital Toronto — offers both traditional metal braces and modern clear aligner systems for children, teenagers, and adults.

Early orthodontic evaluation is recommended for children from age 7, while treatment typically begins between 10–14 years when most permanent teeth are present. Adult orthodontics is equally effective; it is never too late to achieve a straighter, healthier smile. Properly aligned teeth are also easier to clean, reducing the risk of gum disease and tooth decay long-term.

Treatment duration varies from 12 to 24 months depending on the severity of misalignment. We offer competitive, transparent pricing and flexible payment options to make orthodontic care accessible to every family in Kashmir.

Dental Implants in Srinagar

Single Implants · Full-Arch · Implant-Supported Bridges

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to serve as artificial tooth roots, providing a permanent, stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures. Unlike removable dentures, implants fuse with the bone through a process called osseointegration, making them feel and function exactly like natural teeth.

Dr. Sameer Malik, a Member of the Indian Society of Oral Implantology, performs implant surgery with precision using modern imaging and surgical guides. The procedure involves placing the implant, allowing 3–6 months for healing and osseointegration, then attaching a custom-made ceramic crown that matches the colour and shape of surrounding teeth perfectly.

Dental implants have a success rate exceeding 95% and can last a lifetime with proper care. They also preserve jawbone density, preventing the sunken facial appearance that often accompanies tooth loss and conventional dentures.

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) in Srinagar

Endodontic Therapy · Pain-Free · Single-Visit RCT Available

Root canal treatment (RCT) is an endodontic procedure that removes infected pulp tissue from inside a tooth, cleans and disinfects the root canals, and seals them to prevent reinfection. This saves the natural tooth from extraction, which is always the preferred option for maintaining long-term oral health and bite function.

Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is virtually painless — comparable to having a filling placed. Advanced rotary instruments and apex locators at our clinic ensure thorough cleaning even in curved or calcified canals. Most treatments are completed in one to two visits, and the tooth is then restored with a crown to protect it from fracture.

Signs you may need a root canal include severe toothache, prolonged sensitivity to hot or cold, swollen gums near a tooth, or a darkened tooth. Early treatment prevents the spread of infection to surrounding teeth and bone. Dr. Nida Lanker, our general dentist with specialised training in oral surgery, handles complex endodontic cases with expertise.

Professional Teeth Whitening in Srinagar

In-Office Whitening · Take-Home Kits · Laser Whitening

Professional teeth whitening can brighten your smile by 4–8 shades in a single appointment. Unlike over-the-counter whitening strips, our in-office whitening uses clinically proven, high-concentration peroxide gels under careful supervision, ensuring safe, consistent, and dramatic results without damaging your enamel.

Teeth become stained over time due to coffee, tea, tobacco, red wine, and natural aging. Whitening is most effective on extrinsic (surface) stains and is a completely reversible cosmetic procedure. For intrinsic stains or discolouration that doesn't respond to whitening, we may recommend porcelain veneers as an alternative.

We also offer customised take-home whitening trays that you can use at your convenience for gradual, long-lasting results. Our dental team will assess your teeth before recommending the most suitable whitening protocol for your individual case.

Dental Crowns & Bridges in Srinagar

Zirconia Crowns · Porcelain Fused to Metal · Full-Ceramic Bridges

A dental crown is a cap placed over a damaged, weakened, or heavily restored tooth to restore its shape, size, strength, and appearance. Crowns are commonly used after root canal treatment, for broken teeth, or to improve the aesthetics of misshapen or discoloured teeth. Modern zirconia and all-ceramic crowns are indistinguishable from natural teeth and offer exceptional durability.

A dental bridge fills the gap left by one or more missing teeth using artificial teeth (pontics) anchored to crowns on the adjacent teeth. Bridges restore your ability to chew, speak properly, and maintain facial structure. Implant-supported bridges eliminate the need to alter adjacent healthy teeth and are the gold standard for multi-tooth replacement.

Preventive & General Dentistry

Scaling · Polishing · Extractions · Oral Cancer Screening

Preventive dentistry is the cornerstone of oral health. Regular dental check-ups (every 6 months) and professional cleaning (scaling and polishing) remove tartar build-up that brushing alone cannot address, preventing gum disease, bad breath, and tooth loss before they start. Early detection of cavities, gum problems, and even oral cancer during routine examinations saves time, money, and discomfort.

Our general dental services include tooth extractions (including wisdom tooth removal), fillings with composite (tooth-coloured) resin, oral hygiene instruction, dietary counselling, and night guards for bruxism (teeth grinding). We screen every patient for signs of oral cancer at each check-up — a quick, painless examination that can be life-saving.

Is root canal treatment painful?
Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is virtually painless. Most patients report that the procedure feels no different from having a filling placed. You may experience mild soreness for 2–3 days after the treatment, which is easily managed with over-the-counter pain relief. The pain relief from treating an infected tooth far outweighs any minor post-procedure discomfort.
How long do dental implants last?
Dental implants are designed to be a permanent solution. The titanium implant post can last a lifetime with proper care, while the crown on top typically lasts 15–25 years before requiring replacement due to normal wear. Good oral hygiene, regular dental check-ups, and avoiding habits like teeth grinding ensure maximum implant longevity. Implants have a 95%+ success rate worldwide.
At what age should my child first visit a dentist?
The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry and Indian Dental Association both recommend a child's first dental visit by their first birthday, or within six months of the eruption of the first tooth. Early visits help detect problems early, provide oral hygiene guidance for parents, and familiarise children with the dental environment — eliminating dental anxiety before it develops.
Does teeth whitening damage enamel?
Professional teeth whitening performed by a dentist is safe for tooth enamel when done correctly and at appropriate intervals. Over-whitening or using unregulated products can cause sensitivity and enamel damage. At Access Dental Care, we assess your suitability for whitening beforehand and use clinically proven concentrations. Mild, temporary sensitivity after treatment is normal and resolves within 48 hours.
